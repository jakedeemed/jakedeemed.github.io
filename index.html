About

Who I Am.

Hello hello hello! Jake Dotts here 🖖... Welcome to my little slice of the internet! Glad you could drop in and check things out.

I originally hail from Pennsylvania where I started a small business after dropping out of college, then closed shop and went back to school... realizing you can never learn too much and wanted to make a shift. I majored in information science and minored in entrepreneurship and innovation from Penn State in 2016. I then started my career with GE Renewable Energy traveling around for 2 years to different places trying different things. All leading up to my current role in Boston as Head UX Designer for an entire globally dispersed organization within the Digital Technology arm of GE Renewables. I'm lucky and thankful every single day to be working in a field I love and a space I'm passionate about. Can't ask for much more!

Photography for me has always been a form of expression since I was a tyke. From the camera attachment on my Gameboy to the first iPhone with all of 2MP, I was making what I thought was magic at the time. But am now realizing the photos were no good, but what was good was my passion for it. Although I didnt stick with it thoroughly over the years, it came and went in waves like a good old friend. More recently that good old friend has come to stay, and I must say I have 0 complaints.

Services

What I Do.

From my day job as a UX Designer for GE Renewable Energy, to my many hobbies including photography... One thing remains true - I love to push my comfort zone.

  • UX Design

    My current main breadwinner! I mostly design interfaces and user experiences for wind turbine engineers and I couldnt be happier. Being able to merge a space that sparks my design flame as well as passion for sustainability has been truly an incredible treat.

  • Photography

    It all started as fun with a Moment lens on my iPhone 6 plus, then I graduated to a more substantial setup a few years ago and its been an amazing process ever since. I mainly enjoy shooting outdoors.. landscapes, mountains, the whole bit.

  • The Great Outdoors

    Getting outdoors and hiking, biking, climbing, snowboarding, you name it... its a big part of what motivates me day in and day out. All the work I do indoors on the day to day is my means to get out there on the weekends and vacations.

  • Sustainability

    Trying to make the world a better place day by day from carrying all 20 items I purchased out by hand because I forgot my bad... to picking up trash on the sidewalk and being a vegitarian for the past 4 years. Just trying to make an impact any and every way I can.
